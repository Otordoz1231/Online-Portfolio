I have always been fascinated by computers, especially online games. Growing up as an introverted person, I would spend hours playing video games—sometimes an entire day. This hobby led me to love all things related to computers. My favorite quote is “Technology is best when it brings people together.”

One of the biggest challenges I faced was overcoming self-doubt. There were times when I questioned my abilities, but through persistence and the support of those around me, I gained confidence, which led me to pursue computer engineering.

Today, I continue to refine my computer skills, exploring and discovering things that will be helpful throughout this journey. I want to help people, build innovative technology, and bring my dreams and ideas to life.
